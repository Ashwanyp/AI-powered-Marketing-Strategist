I don't just hire marketers‚ÄîI build **revenue-generating machines** disguised as marketing teams. Here's my proven system:

### üéØ **The Foundation: Revenue-First Hiring**
I hire analytical minds who understand business fundamentals first, marketing tactics second. My best performers have been:
- **Former Engineers** who learned marketing and bring systematic thinking
- **Ex-Consultants** who understand business problems before marketing solutions  
- **Sales Veterans** who moved to marketing and understand the full funnel
- **Data Analysts** who discovered they love customer psychology

### üìö **The Onboarding: 30-Day Revenue Immersion**

**Week 1-2: Customer Reality Check**
- Shadow 10+ customer calls or support tickets to learn problems in prospects' own words
- Analyze 50+ won/lost deals to understand real buying factors
- Interview sales team about what messaging actually works in the field

**Week 3-4: Data Mastery**
- Build their first attribution report connecting campaigns to revenue
- Present findings to the entire team (no hiding behind senior staff)
- Identify one optimization opportunity with measurable impact

**Month 2: Ownership**
- Own a complete campaign from strategy through measurement - full funnel
- Present results to leadership with revenue impact analysis
- Propose next iteration based on data, not opinions

### üèÜ **The Operations: Revenue Team Culture**

**Weekly Pipeline Reviews:** Every marketing person presents their contribution to current quarter pipeline. No vanity metrics allowed.

**Monthly Attribution Deep-Dives:** We analyze every significant deal to understand the marketing journey that led to closure.

**Quarterly Strategy Sessions:** Everyone presents data-driven insights about their area. Junior people often have the best ideas because they're closest to the execution.
